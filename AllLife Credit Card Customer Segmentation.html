Performing K-Mean Clustering and plotting. In the below code, average distortion is calculated. The "cdist" fuction here is going to calculate distance of every point with every centroid of a model. So the minimum will give distance of each point to the nearest centroid, then sum up the distances and take average to get "Average distortion" for each number of cluster.

In [52]:
#Finding optimal no. of clusters
from scipy.spatial.distance import cdist
clusters=range(1,10)
meanDistortions=[]

for k in clusters:
    model=KMeans(n_clusters=k)
    model.fit(data_attr_scaled)
    prediction=model.predict(data_attr_scaled)
    meanDistortions.append(sum(np.min(cdist(data_attr_scaled, model.cluster_centers_, 'euclidean'), axis=1)) / data_attr_scaled.shape[0])


plt.plot(clusters, meanDistortions, 'bx-')
plt.xlabel('k')
plt.ylabel('Average distortion')
plt.title('Selecting k with the Elbow Method')
Out[52]:

From the above graph, it can be inferred that "3" is the transition point of the elbow. So, best option for this data set is to select "3" number of centroids.

Comparison between Heirarchical clusters and K-Means clusters¶

From box plot, both clustering method seem to be agreeing on clusters formed. Both the clusters have grouped customers with high credit limit, high credit cards count and total online visits into one group. Both the clusters have grouped customers with low credit limit, low credit cards count and high calls made into one group. Both the clusters have group customers with relatively moderate credit limit, moderate credit cards count and high physical bank visits into one group.

Recommendations to the bank¶

As per our cluster formations, we can see that the people with high credit card count and high limits prefer online mode of communication. These are the high value customers and must be provided best services. Online banking services must be enhanced, live chat options along with chatbot must be implemented. In case, a chatbot is being deployed, bank should make sure that customers get their queries resolved and appropriate first line support is provided at the instance. After that, customers with relatively moderate credit limit and moderate credit card count seems to be prefer physically visiting the bank. So, the bank should focus on improving the bank branches and provide special training to the bank representatives.
Customer with low credit limits and low credit cards count seems to be liking the phone banking. So phone banking improvements must be considered by the bank as a lowes priority.

So the highest priority should be given in enhancing the online banking, then physical branches and then phone banking.
